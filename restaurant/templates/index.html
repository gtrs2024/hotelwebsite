Our Story

A Philosophy of
Honest Luxury

Nestled at 620 metres above sea level, Elara was conceived as an antidote to noise — a place where guests arrive to slow down and leave having truly tasted something. Our kitchen is led by Executive Chef Arjun Mehta, whose decade at Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe and Mumbai informs every plate.

We source within 200 km of our kitchen. Vegetables from Mahabaleshwar farms, seafood from Ratnagiri fishermen, and single-estate dairy from the Deccan Plateau. Luxury, for us, begins with provenance.
